This site about midicine

MD Lingo was started by medical students, Deep and Vlad, after noticing that patients often had little understanding of their illnesses as well as their treatment options when making life-changing decisions.

We believe there are several things every patient has a right to:
  1. Access to known medical information - day and night.
  2. Access to results of cutting edge research and the latest studies.
  3. And above all, having this information presented in easy to understand English.

With this in mind, we set about creating the site, using only the most respected resources and providing you with links to these sources - the same ones used by physicians all around the world. ​

So click around, explore, and get to know us!
